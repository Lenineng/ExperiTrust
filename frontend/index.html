About ExperiTrust

ExperiTrust is a digital platform designed to help undergraduates bridge the gap between education and employment by transforming academic, volunteer, and project-based activities into verified and measurable work experience. The platform introduces a structured verification process that enables employers to objectively assess graduate readiness beyond traditional CVs. By promoting transparency, fairness, and skills-based evaluation, ExperiTrust supports inclusive hiring and improves employability outcomes for early-career job seekers.

Why Choose ExperiTrust

  1. 01 ExperiTrust levels the playing field for undergraduates by recognizing academic, volunteer, and project-based activities as valid work experience. This reduces bias against candidates who lack formal employment history and promotes more inclusive recruitment practices.
  2. 02 The platform introduces a structured verification process that ensures experiences shared by students are credible and trustworthy. Employers can confidently evaluate candidates using standardized experiences metrics rather than relying solely on self-reported CV information.
  3. 03 ExperiTrust directly connects learning outcomes with employer expectations by translating education and practical activities into employer-relevant experience. This helps graduates demonstrate job readiness and enables employers to identify suitable entry-level talent more efficiently.
